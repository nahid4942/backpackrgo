🚗 How to Go

Choose the best transportation for your journey

✈️ By Air

Fastest option for distant destinations like Cox's Bazar and Sylhet. Book in advance for better prices.

🚂 By Train

Comfortable and scenic routes to major cities. Book AC seats for long journeys.

🚌 By Bus

Most economical option with frequent services. Choose AC buses for comfort.

🚢 By Launch/Boat

Essential for river destinations like Sundarbans. Book cabins for overnight journeys.

💡 Essential Travel Tips

Important guidelines for a safe and enjoyable trip

🌱 Environmental

  • Don't litter in nature spots
  • Use biodegradable products
  • Respect wildlife and their habitats
  • Support eco-friendly accommodations

🤝 Cultural

  • Dress modestly, especially in rural areas
  • Remove shoes when entering homes/mosques
  • Learn basic Bengali greetings
  • Respect local customs and traditions

🛡️ Safety

  • Beware of fake tour guides and scams
  • Keep copies of important documents
  • Use registered transportation
  • Stay hydrated and carry medicines

💰 Budget

  • Bargain politely in local markets
  • Try street food for authentic taste
  • Book accommodations in advance
  • Carry both cash and cards
